Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 17, 2025

Overview

At Peace Love and Body Work, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our contact form.

Information We Collect

When you submit our contact form, we collect:

  • Name: To address you personally in our communications
  • Email Address: To respond to your inquiry and schedule appointments
  • Message/Request: To understand your needs and provide appropriate services

We do not collect:

  • Payment information
  • Social Security numbers
  • Health records or sensitive medical information (until you become a client)
  • Location tracking or cookies for advertising

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for:

  • Responding to your contact form inquiry
  • Scheduling appointments and service coordination
  • Providing information about our stretching and mobility services
  • Sending occasional service updates (only if you opt-in)

Data Processing & Storage

Contact form submissions are processed by Netlify Forms, a secure third-party service. Netlify is compliant with industry-standard security practices and GDPR regulations.

  • Secure transmission: All data is transmitted over HTTPS encryption
  • Spam protection: We use honeypot fields to prevent bot submissions
  • Storage: Form data is stored securely in Netlify's infrastructure

For Netlify's privacy practices, see: Netlify Privacy Policy

Data Retention

We retain your contact information for:

  • Active inquiries: Until you schedule an appointment or indicate you're no longer interested
  • Client records: As required by professional standards and applicable laws
  • Maximum retention: We review and purge old contact form data periodically (typically within 12 months for inactive inquiries)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request corrections to any inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal obligations)
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from any future communications

To exercise these rights, please contact us through the website contact form or email.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is shared only with:

  • Netlify Forms: For form processing (as described above)
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or legal process

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure form processing with spam protection
  • No storage of sensitive personal information on public servers
  • Regular review of security practices

Consent

By submitting the contact form, you explicitly consent to:

  • The collection and processing of your information as described in this policy
  • Being contacted regarding your inquiry and services

This consent is indicated by the required checkbox on the contact form.

Children's Privacy

Our services and website are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates when changes were last made. Continued use of our contact form after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale (Note: We do not sell personal information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

Legal basis for processing: Your explicit consent via the contact form checkbox.
